# Inbox

Capture everything here first. Triage daily: process Capture, route items
to the right place, and keep Next Actions focused.

## Capture

New items land here. Processed daily by the triage skill — must be empty
after each triage pass.

## Next Actions

The 3–5 items that deserve focus right now. Each is a single concrete step.
If it links to a multi-step project, reference the project file.

## By context

### @computer

Single-step actions that require your computer.

### @home

Tasks to do at home.

### @errands

Tasks that require going somewhere.

### @calls

Calls or messages to make.

## Waiting For

Things you're waiting on from others. Format: `- **Who** — what — since YYYY-MM-DD`

## Someday / Maybe

Ideas and projects you might do eventually. Review monthly.
