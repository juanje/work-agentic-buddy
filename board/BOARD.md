# Board

Kanban board with a curated Next Actions queue.

- New items land in **Inbox**. Triage during standup or when asked.
- **Next Actions** is a short, curated list of what to tackle next — considering urgency, what it blocks, sprint needs, and maintenance windows. Not a backlog (that's in your issue tracker).
- **Doing** is what's actively in progress (WIP limit: 3-5).
- **Waiting** holds items blocked on someone or something.
- **Done** tracks completions. Cleared during weekly review.
- **Parked** holds ideas, tech debt, nice-to-haves. Not urgent, not forgotten.

Item format:

```
- [ ] Short description | added: YYYY-MM-DD
  - Context: why this matters, what it blocks, etc.
```
